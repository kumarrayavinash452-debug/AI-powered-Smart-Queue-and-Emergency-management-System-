##AI-Powered Smart Queue and Emergency Management System


###Project Overview


The AI-Powered Smart Queue and Emergency Management System is designed to improve the efficiency of queue management in publicplaces such as hospitals, 
government offices, banks, railway stations, and service centers. Traditional queue systems often lead to long waiting times, overcrowding, and difficulties
in handling emergency situations.
This project aims to solve these problems by using Artificial Intelligence to analyze queue conditions, predict waiting times, prioritize urgent cases, and provide real-time updates to users and administrators.


###Why We Are Building This Project.

In many organizations, people spend a significant amount of time waiting in queues. During peak hours, managing large numbers of visitors becomes challenging,
and emergency cases may not receive immediate attention.
The main goals of this project are:

1. Reduce waiting time for users.
2. Improve overall queue management.
3. Automatically identify and prioritize emergency situations.
4. Provide real-time queue monitoring.
5. Enhance user experience through smart notifications and updates.
6. Support administrators in making better operational decisions.
